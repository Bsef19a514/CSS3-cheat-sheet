CSS Numerical Units

Absolute Units:

  • px
  • cm
  • mm
  • in
  • pt- points
  • pc- picas

Relative Units:

  • em (Rel to container)
  • ch (rel to width of 0)
  • rem (rel of font-size of root element)
  • vw (rel to 1% of viewport width)
  • vh (rel to 1% of viewport height)
  • % (rel to parent element)
